Our Story

Built by Tradespeople,
for Tradespeople

YardWorkx started in 2018 when our founder, a general contractor, grew tired of unreliable rentals and sky-high prices at big-box stores. What began as a small collection of tools in a single garage has grown into a full-service equipment hub serving thousands of contractors and DIYers across the region.

We knew there had to be a better way — transparent pricing, well-maintained gear, and real people who actually know the equipment. So we built it.

Today, we operate out of a dedicated 15,000 sq ft yard with over 200 categories of equipment, a same-day delivery fleet, and a team of certified technicians who ensure every machine that leaves our lot is job-ready.

What We Stand For

Our Mission & Values

Every decision we make is guided by these core principles.

Integrity First

We quote honest prices, deliver on our promises, and never hide fine print. If a machine isn't ready, we tell you before you book.

Safety Obsession

Every tool passes a rigorous multi-point inspection before it goes out. Our maintenance standards exceed OEM recommendations.

Community Driven

We partner with local contractors, sponsor job-site training, and reinvest in the trades that build our neighborhoods.

Relentless Reliability

Late equipment costs you money. We maintain a 98% on-time delivery rate and keep backup units ready for emergencies.

Constant Innovation

From online booking to QR-code pickup, we're constantly improving the rental experience with smart technology.

Sustainable Practices

We're transitioning to electric equipment, recycle all fluids and parts, and optimize routes to minimize our carbon footprint.
